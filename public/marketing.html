What is Artificial Eye?

Artificial Eye is an innovative app that uses artificial intelligence to analyze images, recorded videos, or real-time video streams. The app processes visual content and generates detailed text outputs, making it a powerful tool for accessibility, productivity, and entertainment.

Key Features

  • Real-time AI analysis for images and videos.
  • Supports camera and gallery input seamlessly.
  • Generates accurate and comprehensive text descriptions.
  • User-friendly interface with high-speed processing.
  • Optimized for iOS devices for smooth performance.
